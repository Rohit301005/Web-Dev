How To Become A Fronted Developer

What is fronted devloper?

A front-end developer builds the **visual and interactive parts** of a website that users directly see and interact with. They use **HTML** for structure, **CSS** for styling, and **JavaScript** for behavior. Their job is to turn designs into responsive, user-friendly web pages that work smoothly across different devices and browsers. They handle layouts, navigation, forms, animations, and communication with back-end APIs.

A good front-end developer also focuses on performance, accessibility, clean code, and consistent user experience. In short, they create everything the user touches on a website, ensuring it looks good, loads fast, and behaves correctly.
